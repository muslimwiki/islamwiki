# Template:Good article

This template indicates that an article has been reviewed and meets the criteria for a "good article" on IslamWiki.

## Usage

```markdown
{{Good article}}
```

## Parameters

This template takes no parameters.

## Example

```markdown
{{Good article}}
```

## Output

<div class="template article-quality">
    <i class="fas fa-star"></i> This is a good article
</div>

## What it means

Articles marked with this template have been reviewed by editors and meet the following criteria:

- Well-written and comprehensive
- Factually accurate with proper citations
- Follows IslamWiki's style guidelines
- Provides valuable information to readers

## How to use

1. Add `{{Good article}}` to any article that meets the good article criteria
2. The template will automatically display the appropriate styling
3. Users can click on the template to see its documentation
4. Templates can be edited by going to `/wiki/Template:Good_article`

## Related templates

- `{{Featured article}}` - For articles of exceptional quality
- `{{Stub}}` - For articles that need expansion
- `{{Cleanup}}` - For articles that need editing
